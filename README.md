# ECON1626 Assessment 2: Policy Proposal

This repository contains my Assessment 2 policy proposal for ECON1626.

## Repository Contents

- `README.md`: overview of the project and repository navigation
- `proposal.md`: the main 1,200-word policy proposal

## Topic

This policy proposal examines how artificial intelligence is affecting the Australian labour market, with a focus on entry-level workers and workers in routine office-based roles.

## Policy Client

Deputy Secretary, Department of Employment and Workplace Relations.

## Main Policy Question

How should the Australian Government respond to the impact of AI on employment, tasks, wages, and labour-market inequality?

## AI Use Statement

I used generative AI tools to help brainstorm the structure of the policy proposal, improve the clarity of my writing, and check whether my policy options were clearly explained. I did not use AI as my main source of evidence. I selected and checked the sources myself, and I edited the final proposal in my own words.
